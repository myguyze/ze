# Ze — The Vision

Ze should be Jarvis.

Not a chatbot. Not a search wrapper. A personal AI that knows you, works on your behalf, and gets things done in the world — proactively, across days and weeks, not just in response to a single message.

The prospecting use case is a good example of what this means in practice: you set a goal ("run 15 discovery interviews in 6 weeks"), Ze builds the target list, finds contact details, drafts personalized outreach, sends it, follows up, processes what you learn from each call, and updates its own understanding of whether your hypotheses are holding up.

You shouldn't have to manage Ze doing that. You should just get a Telegram message when something needs your attention.

The phases beyond 11 will be about closing the gap between what Ze is now and that.
